About Us

Serving you since 2015

Monica Wanjohi started MS Properties in 2014 to fulfil her long time passion to empower Kenyans to own land, and develop property and manage their investments without breaking the bank. No one ever has enough money – and even if they do buying, selling and managing property can be murky business, and the process is time consuming. Chasing after tenants to pay rent is not the best use of one’s time. MS takes all these headaches away and is a trusted and tested partner in the property investment industry. MS offers signature products for one buying or selling land, renting or buying home or office space, and for property owners and developers looking for a reliable and professional management partner. With MS you will stretch your limits and come off a winner. Since our inception we have helped hundreds of clients own land and property, and find their ideal rental homes and offices, a testimony to our deeply entrenched value system that places integrity above all other virtues. At MS we believe that "The heavens are the Lord's, but the earth he has given to the children of man".
